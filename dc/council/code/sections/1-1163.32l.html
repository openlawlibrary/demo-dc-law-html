§ 1–1163.32l. Rules.

(a) The Elections Board, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules to implement the provisions of [this part], including:

(1) Procedures for verifying and auditing qualified small-dollar contributions; and

(2) Rules relating to:

(A) The storage, use, or disposition of equipment returned to the Office of Campaign Finance under § 1-1163.32h, which may permit disposition of equipment directly to one or more unaffiliated nonprofit organizations or public schools operating in the District;

(B) The removal or deletion of data in equipment returned to the Office of Campaign Finance; and

(C) The discarding of unusable equipment returned to the Office of Campaign Finance.

([b]) For the purposes of this section, the term "equipment" shall have the same meaning as provided in § 1-1163.32h(e).

(Apr. 27, 2012, D.C. Law 19-124, § 332l; as added May 5, 2018, D.C. Law 22-94, § 2(b)(5), 65 DCR 2847; Mar. 13, 2019, D.C. Law 22-250, § 6(nn), 66 DCR 985.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 22-250: § 10 of D.C. Law 22-250 provided that the change made to this section by § 6(nn) of D.C. Law 22-250 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been implemented.

Section 3 of D.C. Law 22-168 amended section 3 of D.C. Law 22-94, removing the applicability restriction impacting this section. Therefore the creation of this section by section 2(b)(5) of D.C. Law 22-94 has been implemented as of November 7, 2018.

Applicability of D.C. Law 22-94: § 3 of D.C. Law 22-94 provided that the creation of this section by § 2(b)(5) of D.C. Law 22-94 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been implemented.
